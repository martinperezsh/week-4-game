Chaos Emerald Game!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four emeralds below. By clicking on an emerald you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number. You lose the game of your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each emerald is hidden to you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
